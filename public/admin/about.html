About E-Codex Play

E-Codex Play is a secure and transparent online raffle draw and digital shopping platform that allows members to purchase raffle tickets, participate in online draws, and buy products through its integrated marketplace. The platform is designed to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all users through strict verification procedures and transaction monitoring.

All deposits, withdrawals, prize claims, and purchases are subject to a two-tier approval process before they are reflected in a member's account dashboard. This process is intended to ensure transaction accuracy, prevent fraudulent activities, and maintain the integrity of the platform.

To comply with security and validation requirements, users may be asked to provide personal information and supporting documents during account registration, deposits, withdrawals, and prize claims. E-Codex Play is committed to protecting all submitted information and handling it responsibly.

Beyond entertainment, E-Codex Play is dedicated to creating a positive social impact. Through its community programs, charitable initiatives, and outreach activities, the platform aims to support individuals and communities in need. Updates regarding these activities will be regularly published through the Events and Announcements section of the platform.

Our Goal

The goal of E-Codex Play is to provide a fair, secure, and reliable online platform where members can participate in raffle draws, purchase products, and enjoy a transparent digital experience. The platform is committed to ensuring that every transaction and raffle activity is conducted with integrity, accountability, and fairness.

E-Codex Play also seeks to build a responsible community by supporting charitable causes and community development programs. Through the support of its members, the platform aims to create opportunities that extend beyond entertainment and contribute to meaningful social initiatives that benefit people in need.

Our commitment is to maintain trust, enhance security, prevent fraud, protect user information, and continuously improve the services we provide to our growing community.
